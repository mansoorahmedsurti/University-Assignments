Biography

Khabib Abdulmanapovich Nurmagomedov (born September 20, 1988) is a retired Russian mixed martial artist and former UFC Lightweight Champion. He retired undefeated with 29–0, holding the longest active win streak in UFC history.

Known for his unmatched grappling, discipline, and respect, Khabib is admired both as a fighter and as a man of faith. His father, Abdulmanap Nurmagomedov, was his lifelong coach and mentor.

Career Highlights
